The process
Your journey, step by step
1
Already done

You've seen your demo

We built you a custom demo site based on your business before you even committed. What you saw is just the starting point. The final version will be fully tailored to you.

2
Day 1

You send us your details

We'll ask you for a few things to make the site completely yours. No long forms, no back-and-forth. Just a quick message with what we need.

  • Your logo (or we'll use your business name styled up)
  • Any photos of your work (phone shots are fine)
  • Exact services you want listed
  • Opening hours and service area
  • Any specific wording you want included
3
Days 2–3

We build your full site

We take everything you've sent and build out the complete, polished website. Every section written properly, every detail in place. Not a template with your name dropped in.

  • Full copywriting for every section
  • Your real photos added throughout
  • Contact form connected and tested
  • Mobile-optimised layout
  • Fast loading speed
4
Day 3–4

You review and approve

We send you a preview link. You check it over, tell us anything you'd like tweaked, and we make the changes. We don't go live until you're happy.

  • Up to 3 rounds of revisions included
  • Review on any device: phone, tablet, desktop
  • We action changes same day where possible
5
Day 5

Your site goes live

We connect your domain, set up hosting, and make the site live. From this point on, anyone searching for your trade in your area can find you.

  • Domain connected (or purchased if needed)
  • Secure HTTPS set up automatically
  • Fast global hosting included
  • We confirm everything is live and working
6
Week 2

SEO foundations added

Once your site is live, we do a proper SEO pass. This is what tells Google exactly who you are, where you work, and what you do. You start appearing in local searches.

  • Local business schema added (Google reads this directly)
  • Page titles and meta descriptions optimised for your location
  • Headings structured around local search terms
  • Image descriptions added for accessibility and Google
  • Submitted to Google Search Console
  • Open Graph tags so links look great when shared
7
Ongoing

We keep things running

Your site doesn't get forgotten once it's live. We're available for updates, changes, and anything that comes up. Your site always reflects where your business is at.

  • Content updates whenever you need them
  • Hosting and security managed for you
  • New photos or services added on request
  • WhatsApp us directly. No tickets, no delays
